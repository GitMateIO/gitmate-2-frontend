GitMate Press Kit

This page explains how you can use our logo and/or write about GitMate.io in public.

Textual Usage

Always spell GitMate with the proper casing.

When using GitMate in without context, use GitMate.io to make it easy for people to understand what this is about.

By the way - I just found GitMate.io, a tool that automates issue triaging.

When referencing GitMate in longer texts with proper context, the .io is cumbersome and to be omitted. Especially if it is contextually clear what you refer to, the explicit reference is not needed.

Historically, the GitMate team has been working on automating a plethora of tedious tasks. To provide a focused product, they decided to focus on managing issues by applying their experiences with machine learning algorithms.

Logo Usage

To use our logo on a light background, use our Inkscape SVG (vector) or our PNG. If you can, a dark background is preferred however: use our Inkscape SVG (vector) or our PNG.

You can download our plain logo as Inkscape SVG (vector) or as PNG. If possible, use Roboto Regular and state our full domain in proper casing: GitMate.io. Ideal background colors are #173542 or #ffffff.

Print Material, esp. Stickers and Shirts

If you want to print GitMate.io stickers or shirts, always include the domain to give context. Use our Inkscape SVG (vector) or our PNG. The sticker should ideally be round cut and there should be no white border around the blue circle.

For shirts, use our logo with our domain in Roboto (Regular) as stated and linked under "Logo Usage". It is recommended to put the domain to the right of the logo.
